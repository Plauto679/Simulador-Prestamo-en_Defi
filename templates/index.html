Welcome to the Health Factor Game




Instructions:

You have been granted 100,000 pesos,
75% in ETH and 25% in XOC (XOC is a Mexican Peso Stable Coin).
You will now learn how to manage your health ratio.

The health ratio is calculated as:
Collateral provided / Loan taken

You will start with 50,000 of ETH as Collateral and 30,000 as a Loan.
So, you still have 25,000 of each currency to survive.

The XOC Balance (in Wallet) grows 5% each round. You have 10 rounds.

Get as much XOC as possible to win!
